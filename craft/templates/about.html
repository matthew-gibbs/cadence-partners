About Us

Who we are, and what we can do for you.

Who we are

{% include "_includes/_divider.html" %}

Cadence Partners is dynamic and agile, with over a century of combined experience in executive recruitment, talent acquisition and diversity consulting. We specialise in senior executive and board level positions, using creative search strategies and transparent, auditable selection processes. Our exceptional market intelligence and reach builds the trust of high-performing candidates from a diverse range of backgrounds.

We deliver with a hand-picked team, combining seasoned Cadence Partners consultants with special advisors. We collaborate with sector-expert special advisors, who engage with their networks and act as technical assessors.

Whether you’re a candidate or a client, you will receive market-leading levels of care, including onboarding and feedback – an assurance of our exacting standards and commitment to quality.

What we do

{% include "_includes/_divider.html" %}

As a provider of executive resources for not-for-profits, the healthcare sector, government, commercial businesses and organisations, we understand when our clients undergo transformational change due to budgetary constraints and competitive pressures. Many of them are required to achieve a greater impact despite having smaller budgets and more competition.

We are helping them to achieve this through identifying the best thought leaders to create innovation, the best change experts to implement transformation and the best operational leaders to deliver new methods in service delivery.

With New Clients

{% include "_includes/_divider.html" %}

Throughout your journey, we will work closely with you to understand your immediate needs, short term goals and longer term strategies. We will apply our knowledge of executive talent pools within the sectors we operate and create productive partnerships with our clients. It’s not uncommon for our clients to stay with us for many years over multiple assignments.

We’ll examine your business, your objectives and your goals. Then, we’ll assign you the most suitable, highly qualified people from our staff pool of experts. They will find you the very best creative solutions to your problems so that your business can thrive.

With New Candidates

{% include "_includes/_divider.html" %}

We’ll take time to get to know you.

We add value for candidates with career counselling, interview training, job searching and personal development coaching. When combined as a service within search assignments, each candidate has a valuable opportunity to develop. They leave the process with a positive view of our clients and a long-lasting candidate relationship.
